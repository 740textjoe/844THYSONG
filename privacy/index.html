Privacy Policy

Effective May 2026

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using 844 THY SONG.

1) What we collect

  • Messages you send us, including text and any media you choose to send.
  • Basic phone metadata needed to send and receive SMS or MMS messages.
  • Purchase, gift code, credit balance, and redemption details when applicable.
  • Basic usage, security, and reliability logs.

2) How we use data

  • To reply to your messages and deliver requested content.
  • To manage credits, gift codes, redemptions, and support requests.
  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the service.
  • To comply with legal, carrier, payment, or anti-abuse requirements.

3) Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited information with service providers, such as SMS, payment, hosting, analytics, and security providers, only as needed to operate the service. We may also disclose information if legally required.

4) Data retention

We keep messages, media, credit records, and logs as needed for operations, support, abuse prevention, legal requirements, and service improvement. We delete or anonymize information when it is no longer needed.

5) Your choices

  • Opt out: Reply STOP to stop messages.
  • Help: Reply HELP for help.
  • You may request deletion of account data where applicable law allows.

6) Children

Our service is intended for ages 13 and older.

7) Questions

For privacy-related questions, visit 844THYSONG.com/privacy or text HELP to 844-THY-SONG.

8) Changes

We may update this policy. When we do, we will post the updated effective date on this page.

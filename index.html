What is THY SONG?

THY SONG is a simple SMS experience for creating short, personalized song-style messages. You can text a mood, a memory, a prayerful thought, an occasion, or a few details about someone you care about.

We reply by text with a song-inspired response meant to feel personal, encouraging, and easy to share.

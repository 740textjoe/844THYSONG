SMS Opt-In Policy

This page explains how people opt in to receive messages from 844 THY SONG.

How you opt in

You opt in by texting one of our official numbers yourself. For THY SONG, the official number is 844-THY-SONG, which is 844.849.7664.

You may also continue an existing opt-in conversation by replying directly to our messages, such as replying Y, YES, REDEEM, or another requested keyword.

No unsolicited messages

We do not send first messages to people who have not contacted us. If someone buys or shares a gift code, the recipient must text 844-THY-SONG themselves to redeem it.

What kinds of messages you may receive

  • Replies to messages you send us.
  • Song-style text responses, encouragement, poems, or related creative content.
  • Credit balance, gift code, redemption, and confirmation messages.
  • Help, opt-out, and service status messages.

How to opt out

You can opt out at any time by replying:

  • STOP
  • UNSUBSCRIBE
  • CANCEL

After opting out, we will not message you again unless you text us first and restart the conversation.

Message rates and help

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your requests. Reply HELP for help.

Your privacy

We do not sell your personal information. Limited message logs may be stored to operate the service, prevent abuse, process credits, and resolve support issues.

844 THY SONG™ is opt-in only. No spam. No unsolicited texts.

Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.

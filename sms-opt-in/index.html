SMS Opt-In Policy

This page explains how people opt in to receive SMS/MMS messages from 844 THY SONG.

How you opt in

844 THY SONG is an SMS/MMS service. The service begins when you choose to text 844-THY-SONG at 844.849.7664.

By texting 844-THY-SONG, you consent to receive SMS/MMS messages from 844 THY SONG related to your request, including replies, confirmations, credit or gift-code information, help messages, opt-out messages, and service status messages.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your activity. Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to opt out.

No unsolicited messages

We do not send first messages to people who have not contacted us. We do not send cold outreach, contact-list campaigns, or recurring marketing blasts.

If someone buys or shares a gift code, the recipient must text 844-THY-SONG themselves to redeem it.

What kinds of messages you may receive

  • Replies to messages you send us.
  • Personalized creative text or MMS responses related to your request.
  • Credit balance, gift code, redemption, and confirmation messages.
  • Help, opt-out, and service status messages.

How to opt out

You can opt out at any time by replying:

  • STOP
  • UNSUBSCRIBE
  • CANCEL

After opting out, we will not message you again unless you text us first and restart the conversation.

Message rates and help

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your requests. Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to opt out.

Your privacy

We do not sell your personal information. SMS consent is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Limited message logs may be stored to operate the service, prevent abuse, process credits, and resolve support issues.

844 THY SONG™ is opt-in only. No spam. No unsolicited texts.

Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.
